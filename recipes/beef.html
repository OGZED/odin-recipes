Beef

Description

A simple and delicious meal that can be made with just a few ingredients that is sure to make you full.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 ground beef (1 pound)
  • bell peppers (any color, 1 pepper)
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • green onions (1 bundle)
  • white onion (1 half)
  • sea salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. Pre-heat pan on medium
  2. Chop up peppers and onions
  3. Once pan is to temperature, add olive oil
  4. Add ground beef, peppers, and onions to pan
  5. Break up ground beef and start to mix ingredients together
  6. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to taste
  7. Continue mixing ingredients so seasonings are distributed evenly
  8. Cook until beef is golden brown
  9. Plate and serve.
